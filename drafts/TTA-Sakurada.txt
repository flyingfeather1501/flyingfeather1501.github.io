The "reset" in Sakurada Reset is not actually travelling through time. Similar to Quicksave / Quickload, it reconstructs the world to the previously saved state. Haruki's Reset is effectively a Quicksave ability with only one slot, that expires in 3 days and has a cooldown of 1 day.
